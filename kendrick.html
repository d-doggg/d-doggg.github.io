Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick started making music at 17 with the rap name "K-Dot". These mixtapes got the attention of Dr Dre, and the next year he signed with the record label "Top Dog Entertainment". He has won 17 grammys and has made over 80 songs. His songs are loved so much for many reasons, but mainly his ability to make the listener truly feel what he is singing.
