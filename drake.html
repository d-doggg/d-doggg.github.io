Drake

Drake became famous at 15 years old when he starred in the Canadian TV show "Degrassi". He then dropped out of high school to pursure acting, but started to write rap songs which were his true intrest. Drake released his first song in 2007 but started gaining popularity in 2009 after releasing "Best I Ever Had". He has now made over 400 songs and is loved all over the world.
